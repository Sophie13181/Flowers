Oleander

Toxic Parts: All parts (leaves, flowers, stems)

Danger: Contains cardiac glycosides – can cause heart failure

Origin: Mediterranean region

Despite being a favorite ornamental shrub, Nerium oleander is one of the most toxic plants on Earth. Even small amounts can be fatal to humans and animals alike.
