Red Spider Lily

Toxic Parts: Bulbs

Danger: Contains lycorine – causes nausea, vomiting, paralysis

Origin: East Asia

With its elegant, fire-red petals and delicate filaments, Lycoris radiata is often associated with farewell and death in Japanese folklore. Though beautiful, its bulb contains potent alkaloids that can be harmful when ingested.
