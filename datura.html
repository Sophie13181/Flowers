Jimsonweed

Toxic Parts: Seeds, leaves

Danger: Contains tropane alkaloids – hallucinogenic and fatal in overdose

Origin: Americas, now worldwide

This trumpet-shaped flower is often seen in wild fields and gardens. Datura has been used in rituals but is extremely poisonous. Its white or purple blooms hide potent toxins.
